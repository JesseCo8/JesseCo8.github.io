About Me

I’m Jesse Lang, an Electrical Engineer with more than a decade of experience designing and building automated test equipment, along with several years in product design. Over the years I’ve worked across hardware, software, and system integration, giving me a broad toolkit for solving tough engineering problems.

My work often sits at the crossroads of hardware and software: developing automation in C#, Python, and LabVIEW; laying out circuits in Altium Designer and KiCad; or verifying and qualifying new designs. What I enjoy most is troubleshooting complex problems and creating solutions that make products more reliable, speed up development, and simplify future work.

I’m open to new opportunities and connections that let me contribute my skills and keep learning.
